Why Use Health@Home?

Health@Home allows you to know what type of OTC's (over the counter) medicines you need without having to go to a doctor or pharmacy. We even give you the dosage you need based off of your age and weight. All this can be done in a matter of minutes. Now there is no need for that dreaded trip to the doctor, or that drive to your pharmacy. With just a few clicks you can find out everything needed based off your common symptoms.

What Is Our Mission?

Our users come first. We built a free website that allows you to get the dosages of the medicines you need. With this cost-effective way, you can use the money you were planning to spend going to the doctor or on gas, for something else. We always look out for you and with this website, we want to make your time the most efficient it can possibly be.

How Does Our Website Work?

Health@Home has input your age, weight and symptom then recommends a medication. We use information from actual pharmacists to calculate dosages. This is based off an I/O (input output) system so it does not hold any data. Note: if you have severe symptoms, please see a doctor as we are not professional.
